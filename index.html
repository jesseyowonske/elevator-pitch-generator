Proposal for Touch Screen Implementation

For the University of Pittsburgh's Film Studies Department.

Conjecture

The problem is that the film studies department is not getting as much interest in their projects, events, and faculty accomplishments as they might want. By designing a user-friendly interface, anyone would be able to easily navigate features i.e., a calendar of upcoming events.

Definition

One possible feature of the interface would be a calendar that integrates touch-swipe support. The main feature will be to have a swipeable list of faculty that when clicked on will show that faculty member’s current project.(Social Media Board)

Quality

The film department is looking for someone to create a touch screen UI to advertise their projects. If we don’t do anything, then the film department doesn’t get any more advertising than they currently do.

Policy

First, we plan to consult with the University of Pittsburgh Film & Media Studies department to determine the specifics of this project. Next, we will make an outline on paper of how our interface will work. Finally, we will code and test our interface and work on the graphics of it.
